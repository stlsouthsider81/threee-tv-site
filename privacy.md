We collect a limited amount of information needed to operate and improve the Service. The Service does not require you to create an account, and we do not intentionally collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information through the website.

**Analytics and diagnostics (Firebase).** The mobile application uses Firebase services provided by Google, which may collect:

- App usage events and screen views
- Device identifiers (such as advertising identifiers or instance IDs), device model, OS version, and language

We do not sell your personal information.
